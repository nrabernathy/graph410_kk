Our Story

Sweet Tooth Cupcakes is a cupcake shop and bakery with three locations throughout Southeastern Michigan. Sweet Tooth cupcakes are gourmet cupcakes baked fresh daily, using real butter, real eggs, and real milk. With over 40 different creatively indulgent flavor offerings daily, there is sure to be a flavor for everyone. Our goal is to get our delectable cupcakes in the mouths of people across the country, and allow them to enjoy the happiness of eating a Sweet Tooth cupcake.

Vanilla

  • Funfetti

    • Our moist yellow cake sweetened with marshmallow fluff and sprinkles, topped with our delicious marshmallow-vanilla buttercream and sprinkles.

    • Our moist cinnamon-vanilla cake topped with brown sugar buttercream and dusted with cinnamon sugar.

Fruity
